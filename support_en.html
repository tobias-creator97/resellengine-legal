Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a product?

  1. Tap "Start new scan" on the home screen
  2. Photograph the product (front + label recommended)
  3. The AI identifies the product and estimates the market price automatically

How do I connect my eBay account?

  1. Settings → eBay Account → Connect
  2. You'll be redirected to eBay's sign-in page
  3. Sign in with your eBay credentials
  4. Allow ResellEngine AI access

How do I publish a listing to eBay?

  1. Scan a product or open an existing draft
  2. Review and edit title, description, price, and item specifics
  3. Tap "Publish to eBay"

What fees apply?

ResellEngine AI itself doesn't charge any publishing fees. eBay charges its own insertion fees and final value fees. Details: eBay seller fees.

How do I delete my data?

  • Drafts: Settings → Delete all drafts
  • eBay connection: Settings → eBay Account → Disconnect
  • All data: Settings → Delete account, or uninstall the app

How does the subscription work?

We offer three tiers:

  • Free: 5 scans per month
  • Pro: 50 scans per month
  • Power: 500 scans per month

Billing is handled by your Apple account. Manage or cancel anytime in your Apple ID settings → Subscriptions.

My scan isn't working — what should I do?

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Force-quit and restart the app
  3. Ensure photos are sharp and well-lit
  4. If the issue persists: email us at resell.engineai@gmail.com with a photo and description
