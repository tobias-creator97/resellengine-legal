## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I scan a product?
1. Tap "Start New Scan" on the home screen
2. Photograph the product (front + type plate recommended)
3. Tap "Start Analysis"
4. The AI identifies the product and determines the market price

### How do I connect my eBay account?
1. Go to Settings → eBay Account → Connect
2. You'll be redirected to the eBay login page
3. Sign in with your eBay credentials
4. Allow ResellEngine AI access
5. Your account is now connected

### How do I publish a listing on eBay?
1. Scan a product or open an existing draft
2. Review and edit the title, description, price, and item specifics
3. Tap "Publish on eBay"
4. Confirm the publication

### What fees apply?
ResellEngine AI does not charge fees for publishing. However, eBay charges its own insertion fees and final value fees. Details at: https://www.ebay.de/help/selling/fees-credits-invoices/selling-fees

### How do I delete my data?
- **Drafts**: Settings → Delete All Drafts
- **eBay connection**: Settings → eBay Account → Disconnect
- **All data**: Uninstall the App
